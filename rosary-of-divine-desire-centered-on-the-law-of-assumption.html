Rosary of Divine Desire: Centered on the Law of Assumption

Feminine, Desire-Based, BBE Version

A Neville Goddard-inspired meditative prayer
Curated to embody softness, receptivity, and the quiet confidence of assumption.

Opening (Introductory Invocation)

At the Cross: Identity
“And God said to Moses, I am what I am.” — Exodus 3:14

First Bead: Receiving

“As you have had faith, so let it be done to you.” — Matthew 8:13

Next Three Beads (Prepare the Soil)

“Let your heart be glad in the Lord; and he will give you the desires of your heart.” — Psalm 37:4
“God is love, and everyone who has love is a child of God and has knowledge of God.” — 1 John 4:7
“Be changed and made new in mind.” — Romans 12:2

Before First Decade (Set the Mood)

“The kingdom of God is among you.” — Luke 17:21

Five Decades: The Soft Power of Desire

Each decade includes a central verse (the “Desire Declaration”) and ten affirming verses (“Petals”), drawing out the feminine themes of receptivity, faith, incubation, and fulfilment.

Decade 1: I Am the One Who Has

Desire Declaration:
“Whatever you make a request for in prayer, have faith that it has been given to you, and you will have it.” — Mark 11:24

Petal (Repeat 10 times):
“He takes delight in those whose hope is in his mercy.” — Psalm 147:11

Decade 2: I Am Already Blessed

Desire Declaration:
“He has made us certain of the things for which we are hoping.” — Hebrews 11:1

Petal (Repeat 10 times):
“Happy will she be who had faith that the things which the Lord has said to her will be done.” — Luke 1:45

Decade 3: I Am the Living Garden

Desire Declaration:
“God gives names to things which have no being, as if they were.” — Romans 4:17

Petal (Repeat 10 times):
“The seed is in itself.” — Genesis 1:11
(Neville: All creation is self-contained imagination.)

Decade 4: I Am the Chosen Vessel

Desire Declaration:
“You did not take me, but I took you, so that you might go and give fruit.” — John 15:16

Petal (Repeat 10 times):
“He will give you the secret desires of your heart.” — Psalm 37:4

Decade 5: I Am Fulfilment in Bloom

Desire Declaration:
“Let it be done for you as you have said.” — Matthew 15:28

Petal (Repeat 10 times):
“As he is, so are we in this world.” — 1 John 4:17

Closing Benediction: The Quiet Flowering of the Wish

“I have done the work which you gave me to do.” — John 17:4
“Let the feeble say, I am strong.” — Joel 3:10
“Your faith has made you well; go in peace.” — Luke 8:48
