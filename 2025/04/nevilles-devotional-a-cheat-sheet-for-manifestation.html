Neville’s Devotional: A Cheat Sheet for Manifestation

In the transformative journey of personal growth and manifestation, Neville Goddard’s teachings reveal that imagination is not just a tool—it is the very creative power of God within us. The Bible consistently reinforces this idea, reminding us of the power of our thoughts, beliefs, and words in shaping our reality. Here’s an extensive list of faith-inspiring statements paired with Bible verses to guide and inspire your path to creation:

1. "Imagination is the creative power of God within me."

“For as he thinketh in his heart, so is he.” – Proverbs 23:7

2. "I am the source of my reality through the power of my thoughts."

“And God said, Let there be light: and there was light.” – Genesis 1:3

3. "What I imagine, I create."

“And God saw every thing that he had made, and, behold, it was very good.” – Genesis 1:31

4. "I have the power to change my world through my imagination."

“If ye have faith as a grain of mustard seed, ye shall say unto this mountain, Remove hence to yonder place; and it shall remove; and nothing shall be impossible unto you.” – Matthew 17:20

5. "I am that which I imagine myself to be."

“For with God nothing shall be impossible.” – Luke 1:37

6. "I am the dreamer, and my world is the dream I have made."

“The Lord is my shepherd; I shall not want.” – Psalm 23:1

7. "Every desire I hold in my heart is already fulfilled."

“Delight thyself also in the Lord; and he shall give thee the desires of thine heart.” – Psalm 37:4

8. "I create my own reality by imagining the best possible outcome."

“Call unto me, and I will answer thee, and shew thee great and mighty things, which thou knowest not.” – Jeremiah 33:3

9. "My faith is my power to transform."

“And Jesus said unto them, If ye have faith as a grain of mustard seed, ye shall say unto this mountain, Be thou removed, and be thou cast into the sea; and it shall be done.” – Mark 11:23

10. "What I believe, I manifest into my life."

“According to your faith be it unto you.” – Matthew 9:29

11. "I am one with God, and through Him, I create my life."

“I can do all things through Christ which strengtheneth me.” – Philippians 4:13

12. "I am the cause of my experience, and I create what I focus on."

“The power of life and death is in the tongue.” – Proverbs 18:21

13. "I am the master of my thoughts and the creator of my experiences."

“As a man thinketh in his heart, so is he.” – Proverbs 23:7

14. "My imagination is limitless, and I use it to create my reality."

“Now unto him that is able to do exceeding abundantly above all that we ask or think, according to the power that worketh in us.” – Ephesians 3:20

15. "I am the architect of my future, and I design it with my thoughts."

“Commit thy works unto the Lord, and thy thoughts shall be established.” – Proverbs 16:3

16. "All things are possible for me when I believe."

“Jesus said unto him, If thou canst believe, all things are possible to him that believeth.” – Mark 9:23

17. "My thoughts are powerful, and I am careful with what I imagine."

“Casting down imaginations, and every high thing that exalteth itself against the knowledge of God.” – 2 Corinthians 10:5

18. "I am continuously creating the life I desire through my faith."

“For we walk by faith, not by sight.” – 2 Corinthians 5:7

19. "I trust in the process of manifestation and align my mind with divine purpose."

“Trust in the Lord with all thine heart; and lean not unto thine own understanding.” – Proverbs 3:5

20. "What I dwell on, I become."

“Set your affection on things above, not on things on the earth.” – Colossians 3:2

21. "Through my faith, I shape my circumstances."

“Now faith is the substance of things hoped for, the evidence of things not seen.” – Hebrews 11:1

22. "The Kingdom of God is within me, and I can manifest it in my life."

“The kingdom of God is within you.” – Luke 17:21

23. "Every thought I hold creates my reality."

“For as he thinketh in his heart, so is he.” – Proverbs 23:7

24. "My faith is my creative power, and I use it wisely."

“Have faith in God.” – Mark 11:22

25. "I am the I AM, and all things are possible for me."

“I am the way, the truth, and the life.” – John 14:6

26. "I am worthy of every desire I hold in my heart."

“For I know the thoughts that I think toward you, saith the Lord, thoughts of peace and not of evil, to give you an expected end.” – Jeremiah 29:11

27. "My mind is aligned with divine intelligence, creating a world of abundance."

“But the wisdom that is from above is first pure, then peaceable, gentle, and easy to be entreated, full of mercy and good fruits.” – James 3:17

28. "I am a divine creator, and I bring forth my desires with ease."

“And God saw that it was good.” – Genesis 1:10

29. "My words carry the power of creation, and I speak life into existence."

“Death and life are in the power of the tongue.” – Proverbs 18:21

30. "I choose to see the world as I desire it to be."

“So that we may boldly say, The Lord is my helper; and I will not fear what man shall do unto me.” – Hebrews 13:6

31. "I am aligned with divine flow, and all things are unfolding perfectly."

“All things work together for good to them that love God.” – Romans 8:28

32. "I am the living expression of God's imagination."

“And the Lord God formed man of the dust of the ground, and breathed into his nostrils the breath of life; and man became a living soul.” – Genesis 2:7

By holding these powerful affirmations in your heart, you align yourself with the divine creative force within you. Every thought, belief, and word has the potential to shape your reality. Remember, you are a master creator—your imagination is the canvas, and God’s power is the brush.
